Administrative details

  • Client: Kråkeluren
  • Contact: Ferdinand Tomek Marnburg

Our client is Ferdinand, the manager (so-called "Crow-Father") of Kråkeluren. Kråkeluren is the "official, unofficial" male chorus of Spanskrøret, the student organization for science lector studies. Ferdinand leads the management board of the troupe, and will be our primary middle-man between the Kråkelur board and our group as we develop their site.

Purpose and goals

The purpose of the website is to make Kråkeluren seem like great choice for new students of Spanskrøret who wants to engage in some social group while studying at NTNU. We wish to create a welcoming and fun website that presents Kråkeluren as an exiting and fun group to participate in.

Audience

The intended audience are male members of the student organization Spanskrøret who wants to be a part of a fun group. Especially guys who wants to take a break from studying and enjoy good company and fun songs. The audience does not need to be guys who are well experienced with singing in a choir, but it is intended to be everyone who would like to learn singing and simply have a good time.
