P1 Project Requirements

Group 33 - Adrian, Cathrine, Henning, Joakim, Martin

Administrative details

  • Client: Kråkeluren
  • Contact: Ferdinand Tomek Marnburg

Our client is Ferdinand, the manager (so-called "Crow-Father") of Kråkeluren. Kråkeluren is the "official, unofficial" male chorus of Spanskrøret, the student organization for science lector studies. Ferdinand leads the management board of the troupe, and will be our primary middle-man between the Kråkelur board and our group as we develop their site.

Purpose and goals

The purpose of the website is to display Kråkeluren as a great choice for new students of Spanskrøret who wants to engage in some social group while studying at NTNU. We wish to create a welcoming and fun website that presents Kråkeluren as an exciting and welcoming group.

Audience

While the website will be approachable to anyone curious about them, the main target audience are male members of the student organization Spanskrøret who's considering joining the troupe. Especially guys who wants to take a break from studying and enjoy good and very vocal company. Candidates are not restricted to guys who are well experienced with singing in a choir either: literally the only requirement they claim is "you must be able to make noise!"

The content of the site and how it is organized

Menu:

  • About Kråkestyret

    This page will give information about Kråkestyret, who are the board in charge of Kråkeluren. It will show off each member and describe what each of their roles are about.

  • Newspaper/blog

    A page featuring blog posts written by various Crows. A nice open platform for them to express themselves, but it will be static however (won't be able to upload new posts dynamically).

  • Tour guide

    The tour guide will feature a showcase of past events that Kråkeluren has taken part or performed in (separate from gallery, which is more free-form, and activitycalendar, which is for upcoming info).

  • Gallery

    The gallery will present images of Kråkeluren and the fun things that they do. There will be a short tag on each image that explains where and when the image was taken and on what occation.

  • Songs

    Kråkeluren has a bunch of songs, organized in a pamphlet that will be presented here. The name of the songs and their lyrics will be on this page.

  • Contact-page/Social medias

    The contagt-page will provide the reader with contact information and the social media accounts of Kråkeluren. It will also provide a map of where Kråkeluren's "headquarters" as well as a link to Spanskrøret.

  • About Kråkeluren

    Here, the group itself will be presented. This page will explain what the group does, their traditions and what it is all about. It will also provide information about the history of the group.

  • Kråke-shop

    On the "Kråke-shop" page, the viewer will be able to browse different items that are for sale from Kråkeluren. These items will be presented with an image, the price and some descriptive text. The shop will not offer direct online purchases however; instead giving information on how to purchase/who to contact.

  • Recruitment

    This page will inform how to become a crow and what this entails (could also link to related Spanskrøret sections: LURemus, the "official, unofficial" female chorus; LURveleven, the mixed chorus; and Naborevyen, the mixed revue group).

  • Activitycalendar

    The activitycalendar will be a page with a calendar showing what happens and when in the next weeks for members of Kråkeluren. The events on the calendar will have a short description.

Functional and Non-Functional Requirements

Functional

  • The website shall provide a good first impression about Kråkeluren's concept, showing off their "culture", traditions and what they're up to currently (though baring in mind it will be a static website initially, as part of the project).
  • The website shall illustrate Kråkeluren's role within Spanskrøret and other concrete info such as song lists, board members, etc.
  • The website shall display Kråke-merchandise and inform users of where to get them (no in-site purchases).

Non-Functional

  • Users should be able to access the navigation menu from anywhere on the site (incl. at any level of scrolling).
  • Users should be able to move between different pages with as few clicks as possible (hover to open navigation, avoid need to return to the front page to find something else).
  • The presentation should have a consistent over-arching theme, based on their culture, humour, colour-scheme (based on the logo) and similar elements.

Final location

A friend and fellow crow of Joakim runs website hosting as part of a business, so the website will most likely end up with them as "kråkeluren.no".
