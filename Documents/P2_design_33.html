Purpose, goals and audience

The purpose of the website is to display Kråkeluren as a great choice for new students of Spanskrøret who wants to engage in some social group while studying at NTNU. We wish to create a welcoming and fun website that presents Kråkeluren as an exciting and welcoming group.
While the website will be approachable to anyone curious about them, the main target audience are male members of the student organization Spanskrøret who's considering joining the troupe.

Navigation structure

We want the website to have a straightforward, easily readable appearance (more on this in the next section). Navigation on the site should be visually intuitive with a persistent nav-bar at the top from which the user can access every single page, from any page, in addition to the logo and title placed above the nav bar, which leads to the front-page when clicked on. The number of pages is manageable enough that they can each inhabit this nav-bar without cluttering it (though splicing a couple of the pages together is not out of the question - more on that under "Content").

Page layout and appearance

Firstly: Aesthetic. We want to use a somewhat minimalistic style, using the colours of Kråkeluren's logo as much as possible to create visual consistency; black, slightly dark grey and an off-white. This could be combined a number of different ways (off-white text on black buttons, black text on off-white background, dark-grey section outlines, etc.), allowing us to distribute some visual interest as well.
Second: Layout. As mentioned above, we want a persistent navigation bar, with the logo on the left (and perhaps social media quick-links, situated to the right) just above. When the user is at the top of the screen, this part should draw some attention, while after scrolling a couple inches (abstract amount for the time being), the logo should shrink down and the nav-bar become thinner, to occupy less space while maintaining navigation no matter where the user is on-screen.

Content

  • The homepage: index.html

    This will be the main front-page of the site. This page will have a big picture covering most of the page, a big header and some text. At the bottom there will be a footer, this will be on every page, but is not shown in the images except here.

  • About Kråkestyet: about_styret.html

    This page will have a few paragraphs about Kråkestyret and pictures of all the members, as well as a desciption of them and what they do in the group. We want this presentet on the page as round images with a name in bold writing and a description under each.

  • Newspaper/blog: blog.html

    The blog page will have several posts in different sizes with images and text. They will be placed in two columns with the same distance between each post.

  • Gallery: gallery.html

    The gallery will have a slideshow of images with descriptions at the top, and all images will also be presentet in three columns below. These will also have a descriprion but will be smaller.

  • Songs: songs.html

    Here all the song names will be presented. If the user clicks one of these names, the lyrics of the given song will be shown below the name. If another song lyrics is already showing on the page when you click another, it will close so that only one lyrics will show at once.

  • Contact page: contact.html

    This page will present the contact information for Kråkeluren. It will also display the social media account names with the social media icons beside them. There will also be a map from Mazemap or Google showing the location of kråkelurens "headquarters".

  • About Kråkeluren: about.html

    Here the whole group will be presented with a picture of all the members and a description about Kråkeluren below. The description will also present the history of the group and their traditions.

  • Kråke-shop: shop.html

    The Kråke-shop page will have the items displayed in boxes in two columns. Each will have a picture of the item, a description and the price.

  • Activity calendar: calendar.html

    The calendar has as purpose to inform the visitor of the website about kråkelurens events and upcoming activities. The dates themselves will not be clickable or editable. To avoid confusion; the visitor will be able to click through months and get information about events posted on the given date.

Minimum requirements

The requirement for Javascript applications will consist of the following:

  1. Image Carousel

    As discussed above, the gallery will have a slideshow at the top (see mockup above).

  2. Image Transition

    The "about Kråkestyret" page will have an image transition effect for each board member: when you mouse over their picture, it'll fade into another one.

  3. Interactive map

    The contact page will have a Mazemap widget, showing off their headquarters and regular "gathering spots".

  4. Interactive calendar

    On the activity calendar, you'll be able to click on various dates/events to see more info, etc.

  5. Song lyrics

    On the song-list page, you can click on a song title to view its lyrics.
